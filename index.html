Trey's Movie Reviews

Hi I'm Trey Gewecke & I'm going to be reviewing a couple movies in this page. Today I will get started by reviewing "The Mummy" franchise the Brendan Fraser trilogy and the spinoff "The Scorpion King" will be adressed on here(I'm aware that there are more Scorpion King movies that were straight to DVD but I'm only reviewing theatricly released movies). This review will be spoiler free. So I hope all of you enjoy.

The Mummy Trilogy

Pros

    It's fun time for history buffs
    A new and fun take on a old story
    The first movie blended horror, action, and comedy so well
    The mythology behind the mummy and why he was punished
    Brendan Fraser is very good at portraying the likable every-man type hero
    John Hannah is good as the funny guy
    The set pieces are historicly accurate
    Good humor
    Well done flashback sequences
    Action is well done for the most part
    Well done camera work in the first 2 movies
    The group of good guys are well portrayed
    Good green screen and CGI effects for surrounding features

Cons

    A huge cheese factor in all the final showdowns
    A wierd opening naration
    THE WHOLE 3RD MOVIE
    A iffy preformance from Arnold Vosloo and Patricia Velasquez
    Bad CGI on characters and other important stuff to th plot
    Annoying and at times poorly writen characters
    In the 3rd movie there was too much use of shaky cam
    Some moments where you can't contemplate what is going on
    Stiff acting from almost everyone the whole 3rd movie
    The 3rd movie had almost nothing to do with the 1st two

Overall I really like this trilogy well except the third movie so what I'll do is grade the first 2 movies together and the 3rd movie seperatley

I will give the first 2 movies a B-
As for the 3rd movie I'll give it a D+
The type of people who will enjoy this. This movie if you look at this closley it is obviously made for histoy buffs and I was at one time studying history so this was basiclly made for me and they did a good job for the most part.

The Spin-off Movie

The Scorpion King

Pros

    The Rock is pretty good and brings a certain charisma to this movie
    The late Michael Clarke Duncan is very good in this movie making everything he touches into gold
    The set pieces in this are very good as is the practical effects
    At times good action

Cons

    Very very over the top in almost every way possible for a film
    This movie looked more like a Old Spice & Baby Oil commercial with a bunch of buff dudes and hot chicks
    So much over acting
    Some wierd naration in the beginning
    a lot very annoying, poorly portrayed, and or poorly written characters
    Very ridiculous lines of dialogue
    Very poor green screen and CGI
    Pure ridiculousness with the action most of the movie
    Very very poor acting at times
    This movie looked like a big giant commercial for the WWE's next fight
Over all this movie is bad but definitley not as bad as the 3rd Mummy movie that being said I'll have to give it a C
I think this movie was made for WWE fans
