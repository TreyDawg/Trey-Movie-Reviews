The Mummy Trilogy

The Mummy

"The Mummy" is directed by Steven Sommers & stars Brendan Fraser. This is a remake of a movie originally released 1932 and was released in 1999. This movie was a moderate success with a total box office gross of $152 million.

The Mummy Returns

"The Mummy Returns" like the first movie returns Steven Sommers as director & Brendan Fraser as the star. This is also Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson's first movie where he plays The Scorpion King. This was a even bigger success than the 1st movie making $433

The Mummy: Tomb of The Dragon Emporor

"The Mummy: Tomb of The Dragon Emporor" unlike the first 2 movies is directed by Rob Cohen not Steven Sommers although he did return as a prducer. The only 2 actors who decided to return for this movie from the first 2 movies is Brendan Fraser and John Hannah. One thing I thought was interesting was Jet Li is the villain. This movie was also a huge success making $401 million.

The Spin-off Movie

The Scorpion King

"The Scorpion King" is directed by Chuck Russell & stars Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson in his first lead role. This was released in 2002 right off the success of "The Mummy Returns". This is produced by Vince McMann and the WWE so that was a huge marketing tool like The Rock being in "The Mummy Returns". This was another success making $165 million worldwide

Pros

    The Rock is pretty good and brings a certain charisma to this movie
    The late Michael Clarke Duncan is very good in this movie making everything he touches into gold
    The set pieces in this are very good as is the practical effects
    At times good action

Cons

    Very very over the top in almost every way possible for a film
    This movie looked more like a Old Spice & Baby Oil commercial with a bunch of buff dudes and hot chicks
    So much over acting
    Some wierd naration in the beginning
    a lot very annoying, poorly portrayed, and or poorly written characters
    Very ridiculous lines of dialogue
    Very poor green screen and CGI
    Pure ridiculousness with the action most of the movie
    Very very poor acting at times
    This movie looked like a big giant commercial for the WWE's next fight
Over all this movie is bad but definitley not as bad as the 3rd Mummy movie that being said I'll have to give it a C
I think this movie was made for WWE fans
