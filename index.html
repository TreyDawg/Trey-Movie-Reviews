The Mummy Trilogy

The Mummy

"The Mummy" is directed by Steven Sommers & stars Brendan Fraser. This is a remake of a movie originally done in 1932. This movie was a moderate success with a total box office gross of $152 million.

The Mummy Returns

"The Mummy Returns" like the first movie returns Steven Sommers as director & Brendan Fraser as the star. This is also Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson's first movie where he plays The Scorpion King. This was a even bigger success than the 1st movie making $433
